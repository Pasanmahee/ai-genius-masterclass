## Role Overview

The Technology Specialist is responsible for managing and improving the technological infrastructure of the organization. This role includes troubleshooting hardware and software issues, implementing new technology solutions, and supporting the organization’s technological needs. The ideal candidate will have a solid understanding of information technology and systems, along with a proactive approach to problem-solving.

## Key Responsibilities

- Manage and maintain the organization's IT infrastructure, including hardware, software, and networks.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues promptly and effectively.
- Implement new technologies and systems to improve operational efficiency and security.
- Provide technical support and training to staff to ensure optimal use of technology.
- Oversee the installation and configuration of new IT equipment.
- Monitor IT systems to ensure they meet the necessary demands.
- Assist with the development and implementation of IT policies and procedures.

## Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Proven experience as a Technology Specialist or similar role.
- Strong knowledge of IT systems, networks, and related technologies.
- Experience with troubleshooting and technical support.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills to effectively collaborate with team members and non-technical staff.

## Benefits

- Competitive salary with potential bonuses.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with employer contribution.
- Opportunities for professional development and certifications.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Paid time off and holiday benefits.
