## What is Synthesia?

[Synthesia](https://www.synthesia.io/) is an AI platform that offers a novel approach to video production. This tool allows users to create videos using AI-generated avatars that can speak and act like humans. These avatars serve as virtual narrators or presenters, making it easier and more cost-effective to produce video content.

## Creating Videos with Synthesia

Using Synthesia is straightforward. Here’s how you can start creating your own AI-driven videos:

### Step 1: Choose a Video Template

Synthesia provides various video templates tailored to different needs, such as sales pitches, learning and development videos, and how-to guides. Each template comes with unique branding and aesthetics to match your company’s style.

### Step 2: Insert Your Script

Paste in your script or use generated content, such as the following example sentences about meditation: "Meditation has been shown to have numerous benefits for both the mind and body. Firstly, it can help reduce stress and anxiety by calming the mind and promoting relaxation."

### Step 3: Generate Your Video

After inserting your script, Synthesia will generate a video with an AI avatar narrating your text. This process replaces the need for traditional filming, offering a quick and efficient way to produce quality videos.
