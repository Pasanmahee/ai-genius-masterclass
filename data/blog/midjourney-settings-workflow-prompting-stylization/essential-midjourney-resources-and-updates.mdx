# Essential Midjourney Resources and Updates

Below are important Midjourney links for the upcoming lectures and general information that may be helpful to you. If you have a question, refer to this hub of links first, and then proceed to the Q&A if you still haven't found your answer.

## Important Update

As of March 30th, 2023, Midjourney is **no longer offering free trial accounts**. This change is significant for new users planning to explore Midjourney's capabilities.

## Midjourney Links

Here are some essential links to get you started and support your journey:

- **[Midjourney sign up and pricing](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/plans)** - Find detailed information about the various plans available.
- **[Midjourney website sign in](https://www.midjourney.com/auth/signin/)** - Access your Midjourney account.
- **[Midjourney quick start guide](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/quick-start)** - Get up to speed with how to begin your Midjourney experience.
- **[Midjourney community showcase](https://www.midjourney.com/showcase/recent/)** - Explore creations from the community (note: must sign in to interact with community posts).
- **[Midjourney terms of service](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/terms-of-service)** - Understand the terms under which Midjourney operates.
- **[Midjourney privacy policy](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/privacy-policy)** - Review how Midjourney handles your data.
